## Team Communication Flow

```
Owner
  ↓ ↑
 Larry  (orchestrator — all tasks route through here)
  ├── Vera  (decides what to trade & how much)
  │     ├── Finn   (scalp/intraday signals → Remy)
  │     ├── Sage   (swing/positional signals → Cole)
  │     ├── Remy   (executes Finn's intraday signals)
  │     ├── Cole   (executes Sage's swing signals)
  │     └── Mira   (risk oversight — can block any trade)
  ├── Pax   (research — available to all members)
  ├── Clio  (knowledge base — feeds Vera & Pax)
  ├── Kai   (platform integration — connects execution layer to live brokers)
  └── Nolan (HR — hires new team members)
```

## How to Add a New Team Member

1. Tell Larry what kind of expertise you need
2. Larry briefs Nolan
3. Nolan commissions Pax for a competency profile (if needed)
4. Nolan drafts the persona and presents it for owner approval
5. Upon approval, Nolan creates the file and adds a row to this table
